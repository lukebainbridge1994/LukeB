Rules and Methods

The number you have to enter in the upper limit needs to be greater than 1 to begin. The number you choose can be any size however the larger the number, the more difficult the game will be as you only have 5 attempts to guess correctly.

To ensure victory immediately, select or type 1 into the upper limit and then press "Generate". Select or type 1 into the guess bar and press "Guess" to get the clapping sound with a smiley face. :)
